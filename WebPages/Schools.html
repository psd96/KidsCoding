About the Schools



Kids coding is one of the leading innovators in teaching children coding.

Teaching is done in Schools local to Volunteers that have signed up to the program.

Schools are vital to the program to provide resources such as computers, desks and chairs to students
whilst also providing a comfortable enviroment to learn and excel in.
