About the Schools


Kids coding is one of the leading innovators in teaching children coding.

Teaching is done in Schools local to Volunteers that have signed up to the program.

Schools are vital to the program to provide resources such as computers, desks and chairs to students whilst also providing a comfortable enviroment to learn and excel in.

Not only will the classes help the students, but also the teachers to create better lessons where students can literally make their ideas turn into a reality with the touch of a button.
