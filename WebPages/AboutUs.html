About Us

Our History

Kids Coding was set up in early 2014 in central Reading and aims to provide local children (aged 9-14) with free access to after-school clubs where they can learn the foundations of computer programming. We believe that if you can try, you will succeed, and that is what we ask for from all of our employees and students. We are a charity that runs off of the public. The more volunteers we get, the more we can provide and grow to make the company a national project and inspire the next generation of computer programmers to come through. We believe that Education is broken. Books and a blackboard are no longer good enough for efficient learning. In a world with one on one interaction and the ability to roam freely, the human mind can expand and grow as much as one can wonder, and this is the aim we try to provide for all of our students.
